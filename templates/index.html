Problem Statement

For this project, we are trying to understand how a student's performance (test scores) is affected by variables such as gender, ethnicity, parental level of education, lunch type, and test preparation course.

Data Collection

I will be utilizing a dataset from Kaggle: Students Performance in Exams. The dataset consists of 1000 rows and 8 columns.
